Why do cats make great pets?


  1. CATS ARE QUIET.

    Well, some cats may be a bit noisy, but most of them are silent. The only thing you have to worry about is them knocking over your stuff.

  2. CATS ARE LOW-MAINTENANCE.

    You don't need to take them outside multiple times a day. They also are able to clean themselves. Though it's not as clean as when you groom them, but it's good enough (unless you have long-haired cats, they do need regular grooming :> ).

  3. CATS ARE INDEPENDENT.

    Cats don't require a lot of attention, they're already happy knowing that you are there. Also, they sleep a lot so you don't need to worry about them being too bored.

  4. CATS KEEP PESTS OUT.

    They scare away mice, bugs, or others. You wouldn't need to spend more for mouse traps and other stuff.

  5. CATS LIVE FOR A LONG TIME.

    Cats have an average lifespan of 12 years! And that doesn't mean that they can't live longer than that. This means that you most probably won't suffer a heartbreaking experience of losing your pet.
